# Memory Model

The vault compounds knowledge through layers:

1. **Capture:** `00_Inbox` receives low-friction, unprocessed material.
2. **Evidence:** `07_Sources` preserves immutable imported content and provenance.
3. **Active context:** `01_Daily` and `02_Working-Memory` hold short-lived focus.
4. **Episodes:** `05_Episodic-Memory` records what happened, when, and why.
5. **Durable memory:** `03_Projects`, `04_Knowledge`, and `06_Procedural-Memory` retain verified state, concepts, and methods.
6. **Machine review:** `08_Machine` stages summaries, synthesis, contradictions, audits, and link suggestions.
7. **Compression:** briefs route to packs; packs route to durable notes and evidence.

Knowledge compounds when reviewed outcomes update durable notes and links without destroying the evidence or episode that produced them. “Semantic,” “episodic,” “procedural,” and “dreaming” are organizational metaphors, not separate cognitive systems.
